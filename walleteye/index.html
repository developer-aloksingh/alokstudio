WalletEye Privacy Policy

Last updated: Oct 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you create an account, add transactions, set up budgets, or contact us for support. This may include:
  • Personal information (name, phone number, email)
  • Financial data (income, expenses you type manually)
  • Device information (device type, OS version)
  • Usage data (features used, time spent in app, preferences)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Process and track your financial transactions
  • Generate spending reports and budget insights
  • Send you important updates and notifications
  • Provide customer support and respond to inquiries
  • Ensure security and prevent fraud

3. Data Security

We take data security seriously and implement industry-standard measures to protect your information:
  • All data is encrypted both in transit and at rest using AES-256
  • We use secure cloud infrastructure with regular security audits
  • Regular security updates and 24/7 monitoring
  • Limited access to personal data by authorized personnel only

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:
  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations or court orders
  • To protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property
  • With service providers who help us operate our app (under strict confidentiality agreements)

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time through the app settings or by contacting our support team.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:
  • Access, update, or delete your personal information
  • Export your financial data in CSV format
  • Opt out of non-essential communications
  • Request information about how we use your data
  • File a complaint with relevant data protection authorities

7. Third-Party Services

WalletEye may integrate with third-party services (such as cloud storage providers) to enhance functionality. These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party services.

8. Children's Privacy

WalletEye is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

9. International Users

If you are using WalletEye from outside your country, please note that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in countries where our servers are located. By using our app, you consent to such transfers.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy in the app and updating the "Last updated" date. Your continued use of WalletEye after such changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
